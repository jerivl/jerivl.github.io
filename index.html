About Me

Jeri Luckenbaugh holds the B.S. degrees in Mathematics and Electrical Engineering from The University of Texas at Dallas (UTD) after graduating with honors in December 2021. They are a member of the IEEE-Eta Kappa Nu honor society and previously a recipient of the UTD-Academic Excellence Scholarship. She has previously held positions in both academia and industry at institutions such as the Southern Methodist University and General Motors. Her research interests relate mostly to signal processing, mathematical analysis, and geometry applied to data, with topics including, but not limited to approximation theory, machine learning, and dynamical systems. Her goal is to prove theorems that yield new, data-driven methods of processing real-world information for a healthier, more accessible, and more equitable future.
